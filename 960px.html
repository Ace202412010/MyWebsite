SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities

SDG 10 aims to reduce inequality within and among countries. It promotes inclusion regardless of age, sex, race, religion, disability, or economic status. It also encourages equitable representation for developing nations and the elimination of discriminatory laws and practices.
